About Harmonica

We believe music education should be accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Harmonica uses clear visual guides and proven music theory to help you learn and explore music at your own pace.

Our Mission

Harmonica exists to democratize music education by providing free, interactive tools that make learning music theory intuitive and enjoyable. We harness the power of the Web Audio API to create realistic sounds and engaging visualizations.
